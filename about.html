About Sri Mookambika Agro Foods LLP

Sri Mookambika Agro Foods LLP is an integrated farm-to-pack food processor based in Ranebennur, Karnataka. We specialise in gherkins, pickled vegetables and related ingredient solutions for export markets.

Our Mission

To supply safe, traceable and consistent vegetable ingredients to global buyers while strengthening rural livelihoods.

Our Scale

A certified processing facility, a 30,000 sq ft production area and partnerships with 1200+ contract farmers across the region.

Certifications & Compliance

FSSC 22000 v6, STAR-K Kosher audit completed, FSSAI, IEC and APEDA RCMC. US FDA agent application via UQSR in progress.

Our Farmer Partnerships

We work directly with farmers under contract arrangements. Our field team provides inputs, training, and agronomy support, ensuring on-time supply, quality checks and fair payments. The model reduces intermediaries and improves farmer incomes.

Social & Quality Commitments

No child labour, regular health checks, farmer training sessions, and strict supplier approval processes are core parts of our compliance program. Lab-based testing and batch traceability ensure food safety for exports.

  • 65 confirmed FCL orders (next 4 months)
  • FSSC 22000 and STAR-K Kosher certified
