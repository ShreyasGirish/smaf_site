Our Products

We supply high-quality vegetable ingredients and pickled solutions to global customers. Our portfolio includes Natural Vinegar (NV), Acetic Acid (AA), and Brine (BR) across multiple grades, cut sizes and pack formats suitable for industrial buyers and private label partners.

Core Product Families

Natural Vinegar (NV)

Food-grade fermented vinegar used in pickles, salads, and condiments. Available in grades like 150/300, 300/450 and 80/150.

Acetic Acid (AA)

High-purity food or industrial grade AA, used for acidification, preservation and pH control.

Brine (BR)

Customised brine systems for pickling, fermentation, industrial filling lines and private label products.

Available Pack Formats

HDPE Drums

Most preferred for export. Available in 220L / 260L / 280L depending on customer spec.

Pails

Suitable for medium-volume processors and private-label fillers.

Pouches

Used for retail-ready units and value-added product lines.

IBC Totes

Available on request for large-volume industrial buyers.
