Private Label & Co-Packing Solutions

We support brands, retailers, importers and food processors with turnkey private label and co-packing solutions for gherkins, pickled vegetables, brines and value-added items. Our certified facility and strong farming base allow us to deliver consistent, compliant and customized products.

Why Choose SMAF for Private Label?

Custom Product Development

We tailor media (NV, AA, BR), flavour profiles, cuts (whole, spears, slices) and grades to match your specification or target market.

Flexible Packing Options

Bulk drums, pails, pouches and retail-ready units. Suitable for both industrial filling lines and consumer SKUs.

Compliance & Documentation

Full support for export documentation, FSSC 22000 compliance, Kosher certification, COA, MSDS and batch traceability.

Stable Sourcing

A strong network of 1200+ contract farmers ensures predictable raw material supply, reducing risks for private label partners.

Our Development Process

  1. Requirement Understanding: Customer shares target product or sample.
  2. Specification Matching: We match grade, media, flavour and pack format.
  3. Pilot Samples: Samples are sent for customer approval.
  4. Production & QA: FSSC 22000 compliant batch production.
  5. Packing & Dispatch: Export-ready preparation and logistics coordination.

Value-Added Capabilities

  • Custom cut profiles: whole, halves, spears, slices, chips
  • Brine customisation for acidity, salinity, flavour
  • Retail-ready recipe blends
  • Label design support on request
  • Small-batch trial runs for new markets
